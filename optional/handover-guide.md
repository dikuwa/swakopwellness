# Handover Guide

Provide the owner with:
- Admin login and recovery process
- User/permission guide
- Service and price editing guide
- Booking and follow-up workflow
- Invoice/payment/receipt workflow
- Website content and media guide
- WhatsApp enable/disable guide
- Chatbot knowledge and safety guide
- Backup, incident and support contacts
- Environment/service ownership register

Never send credentials inside the handover document. Transfer them through a secure password manager.
