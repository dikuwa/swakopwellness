# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Public clients in and around Swakopmund use the site on mobile to understand complementary wellness services, prices, safety information and booking options. Internal users include the owner, admins and staff who manage bookings, clients, services, follow-ups, documents, payments, settings and approved chatbot knowledge.

## Product Purpose

Swakop Wellness Centre needs one shared system for public content, booking requests, chatbot-assisted booking, service management, client workflows, follow-ups and financial documents. Success means a client can understand core services and start booking quickly, while staff manage requests, client records, payments and documents from database-backed operational tools.

## Brand Personality

Calm, botanical and trustworthy. The tone is warm, careful and practical, avoiding hospital sterility, generic spa language, cure claims and over-designed SaaS patterns.

## Anti-references

Do not create a static brochure site, medical template, generic wellness stock-photo site, glassmorphic dashboard, excessive-gradient landing page, cure-oriented health product, or hardcoded service catalogue. Avoid long uninterrupted text, browser-default dialogs, generic AI chat widgets and dashboard templates that ignore permissions or financial integrity.

## Design Principles

- Shared truth: business details, services, prices, booking rules, policies, chatbot knowledge and document details come from dynamic data.
- Calm clarity: short sections, visible booking actions and plain safety wording help clients act without pressure.
- Operational trust: protected dashboard surfaces prioritise predictable workflows, permission-aware states and auditable actions.
- Safety before persuasion: complementary wellness wording stays editable, cautious and free of diagnosis or treatment promises.
- Mobile first: public booking, contact actions and chatbot flows must work cleanly at small widths.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Interfaces need semantic landmarks, labelled inputs, keyboard navigation, visible focus, correct heading hierarchy, accessible dialogs and drawers, non-colour-only statuses, reduced-motion support and 320px mobile compatibility.
